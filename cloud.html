What are THC-A Flowers?

+

All (THC dominant) Cannabis is naturally high in THC-A. THC-A is the acidic precursor to THC Delta-9 and naturally forms before Delta-9 during the lifecycle of the cannabis plant. THC-A also converts to THC Delta-9 through Decarboxylation or heat.

What is Apple Tart (Indoor) THC-A Flower?

+

Apple Tart (Indoor) THC-A Flower is a cannabis strain known for its delightful flavor, captivating aroma, and visually appealing buds. It offers a sweet and fruity taste resembling green apples, combined with hints of vanilla and herbal notes. This mix creates a complex and enjoyable experience for users. It stands out due to its well-rounded effects, combining mood elevation, creativity enhancement, and soothing body relaxation.
